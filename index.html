<header> contains introductory content, a logo, navigation aids, etc.
<nav> contains navigation links, either to the current page or to other pages. e.g. table of contents, indexes.
The <body> element contains the content of a webpage.
The <main> element contains the dominant content or main content of a webpage.

A <p> represents a paragraph, which is often a block of text, but may also be used to group related content such as images or form fields.

<caption> specifies the caption or title of a table.
<table> denotes a table, <thead> its header, <tbody> its body, <tr> a row, <th> a header cell, and <td> a cell.
